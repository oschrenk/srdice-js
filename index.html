srdice-js by oschrenk

Optimized for the iPhone/iPod Touch.

Visit an online version of the Dice Roller here

You can choose a number of dice (1-20) and a target number (2-20) and touch the text "Roll the f*** dice" to see how man successes you got.

It also shows the the highest result if you just have to make an Open Success Test and the sum off all dice for an Initiative Roll
